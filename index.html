about kikuyu room

Kikuyu Room is a room between memory, imagination, and daily life.

It is not a time machine, but a vessel for time.

We believe ordinary days deserve beautiful things.

We collect moments that deserve to stay.

what is room?

Room is not only a place.

It holds the toy you kept, the afternoon light on your desk, the bag you carried every day, and the future room you have not moved into yet. In Kikuyu Room, memory and daily life can sit together softly.

a vessel for time

Not a time machine.

We are not trying to go back. We are choosing small objects that help time stay visible: a ribbon, a cup, a memo, a lamp, a quiet piece of clothing.
